Ranking Rules

1. Leapfrog Rule: To overtake a higher-ranked player, you must defeat them twice in a row.
Example: If Player A (Rank 10) beats Player B (Rank 2) twice consecutively, Player A takes Rank 2. Player B and everyone else between Rank 2 and 10 shift down by one spot.
2. Draws do not reset your winning streak against a player, but they do not count as a win.
3. New players enter at the top of the rankings (Rank 1), pushing everyone else down.
